California Civic Engagement Project

Mapping California's Poltically Vulnerable Communities

I created a series of maps with the CCEP to display the relationship between low voter turnout and the economic, educational and health challenges faced by communities in our state. These maps, which include print maps and web maps, were created to help activists and policy makers for a variety of purposes, for example, to identify areas of priority in voter registration efforts or teach students about civic engagement and barriers to participation.

Hot Spot Maps identify communities with overlapping socioeconomic and environmental disadvantages, as well as low voter turnout that result in political vulnerability. These maps present the CCEPâ€™s Index of Political Vulnerability. The index includes education, economic, and environmental health measures commonly associated with community disadvantage and related to low voter turnout; the areas with statistically significant high and low values were flagged and aggregated in the index. OpenBudget

Working with OpenOakland's OpenBudget project to make the city budget process more transparent and accessible to Oaklanders

This web map was created to help citizens attend their local community budget forum by enabling them to quickly peruse the different forum sites and services. The data was pulled from a list posted on the mayor's website.
