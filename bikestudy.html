Cyclist route choice, traffic-related air pollution, and lung function: a scripted exposure study

These figures were part of a study that examined the affect various air pollutants have on commuter bicyclists. As part of the study, cyclists biked on pre-determined routes through the city of Berkeley during peak traffic and low traffic hours. These 3D maps display different levels of pollutants along the route during the different times of day.
