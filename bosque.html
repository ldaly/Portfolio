Forest for a Living: Alleviating Poverty to Conserve Rainforests

Forest for a Living is a conservation and reforestation project that seeks to provide incentives for conservation while supporting the entrepreneurial efforts of the farmers. The project included creating a GIS database of the properties of the participating farmers.
